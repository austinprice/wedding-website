Eat & Drink

When choosing a place to stay, somewhere around Lake Rabun, Lakemont or downtown Clayton would be best but it’s hard to go wrong!

  • Fortify - upscale farm to table restaurant, reservations recommended
  • Fortify Pi - Fortify's more casual spot w/ great pizza and craft beer
  • Universal Joint - big outdoor space w/ great burgers, wings, and beer
  • Grapes & Beans - lunch spot w/ great sandwiches, wraps and salads
  • Tallulah Outdoor Adventures - cafe & bar sharing a space with a climbing wall and other outdoor activities
  • Lake Rabun Hotel - charming hotel restaurant and bar close to the wedding venue
  • Clayton Cafe - classic diner breakfast in downtown Clayton
  • Napa South - wine bar in downtown Clayton

Explore

When choosing a place to stay, somewhere around Lake Rabun, Lakemont or downtown Clayton would be best but it’s hard to go wrong!

  • Tallulah Gorge State Park - scenic falls & overlook w/ several trails to the bottom of the gorge
  • Tallulah Outdoor Adventures - great spot to rock climb & drink a beer (Andrew)
  • Wander - awesome outdoors store in the heart of downtown Clayton
  • Minihaha - very short hike at Lake Rabun w/ a stellar, huge waterfall
  • Picken's Nose - easy/moderate hike w/ a stunning overlook. Long, steep dirt road leads up to the trail.
  • Rabun Bald - second-highest peak in Georgia, step trail w/ incredible views
  • Panther Creek - 7-mile hike w/ diverse scenery along the way
