Eat & Drink

Here are some of our favorite spots to eat, drink and hang out.

  • Fortify - upscale farm to table restaurant, reservations recommended
  • Fortify Pi - Fortify's more casual spot w/ great pizza and craft beer
  • Universal Joint - big outdoor space w/ great burgers, wings, and beer
  • Tallulah Adventures - cafe & bar sharing a space with a climbing wall and other outdoor activities
  • Lake Rabun Hotel - charming hotel restaurant and bar close to the wedding venue
  • Clayton Cafe - classic diner breakfast in downtown Clayton
  • Napa South - wine bar in downtown Clayton

Explore

The best place to be in North Georgia is outside! Here are some of our favorite places to go and trails to hike.

  • Tallulah Gorge State Park - scenic falls & overlook w/ several trails to the bottom of the gorge
  • Tallulah Adventures - great spot to rock climb & drink a beer (this one's for you, Andrew)
  • Wander - awesome outdoors store in the heart of downtown Clayton

    • Hiking Trails

  • Minnehaha [0.4 mi] - very short hike at Lake Rabun w/ a stellar, huge waterfall
  • Pickens Nose [1.4 mi] - easy/moderate hike w/ a stunning overlook. Long, steep dirt road leads up to the trail.
  • Rabun Bald [3.4 mi] - second-highest peak in Georgia, step trail w/ incredible views
  • Panther Creek [7.3 mi] - 7-mile hike w/ diverse scenery along the way
