Lay of the land

Festivities will be held nestled in the foothills of the Appalachian Mountains. This area is very special to Cat and her family (and now Austin, too!). Like many small mountain towns, there aren't many large hotels nearby but there are a lot of lovely Airbnb, VRBO and boutique hotel options.

Parking & Transportation

Parking is not available at the venue but we have arranged shuttles to help with transportation. Please park nearby at the Lakemont Fire Station at 91 Lakemont Cir Lakemont, GA 30552. Shuttles will be available to take you to and from the venue.
